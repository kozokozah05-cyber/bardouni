Privacy Policy

This page explains how the BEN AHMED RENT CAR Android application handles information. The app is designed to work mainly on the user's device and to avoid unnecessary data collection.

What the app does

Offline vehicle catalog, rental estimates, local booking-request saving, and optional export or sharing actions chosen by the user.

02

What the App Does

The app provides an offline vehicle catalog, local rental estimate tools, local booking-request saving, and optional export or sharing actions started by the user.

The app does not process payments and does not automatically confirm rentals. Any rental confirmation happens directly with BEN AHMED RENT CAR outside the app.

03

Data the App Handles

The app may store data entered by the user directly on the device, including:

  • selected vehicle information;
  • rental dates and estimate values;
  • optional notes entered by the user;
  • local pricing preferences, such as extra kilometer cost;
  • an onboarding preference that remembers whether the welcome screen was already shown.

This data is handled locally on the device for app functionality.

04

Data Collection and Sharing

BEN AHMED RENT CAR does not collect this app data on its own servers through the app.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, tracking technologies, or background data collection.

The app does not share user data with BEN AHMED RENT CAR or third parties unless the user intentionally chooses an Android system action such as export or share.

05

Export and Sharing

If the user uses the export feature, the app writes a text file only to the destination selected by the user through the Android system document picker.

If the user uses the share feature, only the text explicitly generated for sharing is passed to the app or service chosen by the user through Android sharing.

06

Permissions and Device Access

The app does not request dangerous Android permissions such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, call logs, SMS, or broad storage permissions.

The app works without account registration and without mandatory internet-based login.

07

Retention and Deletion

Data stored by the app remains on the device until the user deletes it from the app settings or uninstalls the app, subject to normal Android device behavior.

The Settings screen includes controls to delete saved local requests and local preferences.

08

Security

The app stores its local records inside the app's normal Android local storage environment. However, no mobile application can guarantee absolute security on every device, so users should avoid storing unnecessary sensitive personal information in optional notes.

09

Children

The app is not specifically directed to children and is not designed for child-focused services.

10

Changes to this Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version should be published with a new date.
